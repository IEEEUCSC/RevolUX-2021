What is RevolUX ?

RevolUX - A UI/UX designation, unleashing the creative and artistic minds of Sri Lankan undergraduates and schoolers, organised by AIESEC in the University of Colombo in collaboration with the IEEE Student Branch of UCSC.

The major objective of this event is to give exposure on different trends in User Interface and User Experience Design, and related fields to participants, so that they can understand a problem and create a solution in the user's perspective which will cater to their needs.

guidelines

Guidelines

⚙️ Competitors can be schoolers or undergraduates.
⚙️ All the team members should be from a same school or a university. (No grade and batch restrictions.)
⚙️ If you are a candidate who has just finished your A/L's , you should register for the competition mentioning as a "schooler" and you should team up with co-candidates from the same school that you did your A/L.
⚙️ A team should consist of a minimum of 2 members and maximum of 4 members.
⚙️ In the initial round competitors submit UI mockups and prototypes according to given domains.
⚙️ The final round would be only for the selected finalists from the initial round and it would be a 12 hour designathon where the teams should design UI mockups for a given problem/scenario.
schedule

Event Schedule

  • 2nd June - 12th June

    Form a group and get registered

    Once registration is open, form a team in accordance with the guidelines and register your team.
    Register your team for the destination.

  • 5th June

    Opening doors for UI/UX - Workshop 1

    An initial workshop will be organised to give an idea and understanding about UX/UI, it’s job market and new trends.
    Via zoom platform
    Register for workshop 1

  • 12th June

    Explore more about UI/UX - Workshop 2

    This would be another workshop to cover some more concepts of UI/UX to enhance the knowledge of participants.

  • 19th June

    Introductory session

    Once registrations are closed an introductory session will be conducted and the main objective would be to explain how to build a simple UI using different designing tools.
    This is a compulsory session for all competitors who have registered for the competition.
    Via zoom platform

  • 20th June - 25th June

    Submit a proposal

    Candidates have to submit a document defining thier own problem from the one of given domains.
    Candidates have to present a solid UI solution by mockups and prototypes,
    Template for the document will be given to the candidates.
    Top 10 teams will be selected for the final destination from this initial round.
    UIs submitted by these 10 teams will be sent to the most popular category.

  • 3rd July

    Final round

    Final round would be a 12 hour designathon where each team would be provided with a scenario/problem where they have to create UI mockups/prototypes for that given scenario/problem.
    Any design tool, (Adobe XD, Figma, Canva, etc) any language or framework (HTML, CSS, Javascript, React, Angular, etc.) is allowed.

  • 4th July

    Grab the trophy

    A winner, 1st runner up and a 2nd runner up will be selected.
    Winners of the most popular category will be selected by online voting.
